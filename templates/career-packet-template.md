# Career packet template

Copy this directory structure into your private workspace. Do not fill it with private data in this public repository.

```text
<CAREER_PACKET_ROOT>/
  01 - Not Reviewed/
  02 - Interested/
  03 - Applied/
  04 - Interview/
  05 - Offers/
  06 - On Hold/
  99 - Not Interested/
  100 - Rejected/
  DOCX Versions/
```
